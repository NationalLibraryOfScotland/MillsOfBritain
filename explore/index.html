Acknowledgements

Numerous individuals inspired the curation of this database and were indespensible to the creation of this viewer. We thank Chris Fleet (National Library of Scotland) for his tireless assistance with our project and in the construction of this project webpage. The 'Scottish Water Mills Project' viewer, created by Iara Nave Calton, inspired our sister viewer for county map data and will inform our future work. And, lastly, we cannot go without expressing our gratitude to our late University of Glasgow colleague, Paul Bishop, who helped to set this entire work in motion.

Data

These data are free to re-use under a Creative Commons CC-BY license. Data can be accessed in a Figshare data repository or by email request below. When reusing this resource, please cite:

Jonell, T.N., Jones, P., Lucas, A., Naylor, S., 2024, Limited waterpower contributed to rise of steam power in British ‘Cottonopolis’: Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences Nexus, doi: 10.1093/pnasnexus/pgae251.

Note: We strongly advise users of these data for professional research purposes to familiarise themselves with the issues surrounding county maps and mills in our Accuracy and Georeferencing section. This resource is incredibly informative if used wisely!

Updates and Versioning

Due to the (mostly) static nature of this database, changes to this webpage and the underlying data in the viewer may not be made for some time. This website was last updated on 17/10/2024 by TNJ. Database version: Mills of Britain_17291836_v1.
